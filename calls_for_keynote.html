Call for Keynote Talks

It is our great pleasure to invite you to be one of the Keynote Speakers at Interpartner-2019. Keynote speakers are usually given 20 minutes to speech. The speech topics need to be relevant to the conference topics. Some qualities of well-versed keynote speakers include: fluent English language, highly regarded oratory skills, intelligence and background on the topic, ability to engage an audience, creativity, a good sense of humor, strong use of visuals or audio, and openness for sharing personal experiences.

Join us as one of the Keynote Speakers, you can attend this conference with 50% discount, including all privileges as regular authors.  If you would like to join us, please kindly send us your proposal by email  vmt47@ukr.net according to the template.

Deadline for Keynote Talks proposal is July 20, 2019. The decision will be announced as soon as your proposal will be reviewed by Program Committee and agreed by Steering Committee, but not later than August 05, 2019.
