Invitation Letter

Dear Colleagues,

We would like to invite you to submit original and unpublished research articles to the Grabchenko’s International Conference on Advanced Manufacturing Processes (InterPartner-2019), organized by Odessa National Polytechnic University and National Technical University “Kharkiv Polytechnic Institute”, which will be held on September 10-13, 2019 in Odessa. The official website is http://interpartner.odessa.ua.

InterPartner-2019 focuses on promoting research and developmental activities, intensification of scientific information interchange between researchers, developers, and engineers. The conference is an ideal platform for people to share views and experiences in fields of Manufacturing Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Materials Science, and Quality Assurance.

Authors should submit their papers online using the EasyChair Conference Management System. The submission page for InterPartner-2019 is https://easychair.org/cfp/InterPartner-2019.

The deadline for paper submission is June 30, 2019. After the careful reviewing process, all accepted papers will be published and submitted to the database SCOPUS and other indexing services. Accepted papers must be presented at the conference by one of their authors, who commit to register and attend the conference. The Steering Committee reserve the right to exclude papers which have not been presented from the proceedings.

Looking forward to your submissions! 

Best regards,
Prof. Volodymyr Tonkonogyi,
Conference Chair
