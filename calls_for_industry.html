Calls for Industry Talks

Industrial practitioners are invited to submit talk proposals to the Industry Talks Session of the Interpartner-2019. This session seeks presentations about practical solutions and challenges to significant real-world issues facing industrial practitioners. It will provide a unique venue for industrial and academia participants to exchange ideas and experiences. Examples of talk topics include, but are not limited to:
  • techniques that solve significant issues in practice;
  • tool development and problem-solving experience report;
  • forgotten research topics that are highly relevant to industry nowadays;li>
  • new challenges faced by industrial practitioners that need help from research.
If you would like to join us, please kindly send us your proposal by e-mail vmt47@ukr.net according to the template.

Deadline for Industry Talks proposal is July 15, 2019. The decision will be announced as soon as your proposal will be reviewed by Program Committee and approved by Steering Committee, but not later than August 10, 2019.

The content of the talk can be included in the conference proceedings after blinded peer-review by the Program Committee members and the approval  by the Steering Committee.
