Best Presentation Award

One Best Presentation will be selected from each session based on the score of secret voting by all conference participants.

The Certificate for Best Presentation Award will be awarded during the Awards & Farewell Session of the conference (September 13, 2019).

Please note that only presented papers are eligible for Best Presentation Award.

Best Presentation Award will be granted by one of the sponsors of Interpartner-2019.
